## üß† **How to Choose the Right Database (Simple Decision Guide)**

### ‚úî Choose **SQL (Relational)** if:

-   Your data is structured
    
-   You need ACID transactions
    
-   You have complex queries or joins
    
-   You‚Äôre building ERP, banking, HR, finance, P2P workflows, or inventory systems
    

### ‚úî Choose **NoSQL** if:

-   You need high scalability
    
-   Your schema changes frequently
    
-   You store huge unstructured data in real time
    
-   You‚Äôre building social apps, log collectors, IoT, or caching layers
    

### ‚úî Choose **In-Memory DB** if:

-   You need sub-millisecond response
    
-   You want distributed caching
    
-   You are handling rate limits, queues, pub/sub
    

### ‚úî Choose **TSDB** if:

-   You store metrics, logs, events
    
-   You need fast rollups & retention policies
    

### ‚úî Choose **Graph DB** if:

-   Your system relies on relationships (friends, connections)
    

### ‚úî Choose **Distributed SQL** if:

-   You need relational consistency + NoSQL scale
    
-   You operate across multiple regions
